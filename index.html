Centreline Generator

Centreline Generator

Creator: jakcharvat

Type: Web App

Centreline Generator is a tool for generating coordinate for extended centrelines, based on the ends of the runway. The tool calculates the exact magnetic bearing between the two points, and uses that to generate a 25NM dashed centreline with dashes 0.5NM each, and a perpendicular dash every 10 dashes to indicate 5NM.
